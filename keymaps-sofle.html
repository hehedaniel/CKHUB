  * ,-------------------------------------------------.                      .-----------------------------------------------.
  * |    `    |   1   |   2   |   3   |   4   |   5   |                      |   6   |   7   |   8   |   9   |   0   |   `   |
  * |---------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------|                      |-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------|
  * |   ESC   |   Q   |   W   |   E   |   R   |   T   |                      |   Y   |   U   |   I   |   O   |   P   |  Bspc |
  * |---------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------|                      |-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------|
  * |   Tab   |   A   |   S   |   D   |   F   |   G   |--------.    .--------|   H   |   J   |   K   |   L   |   ;   |   '   |
  * |---------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------|        |    |        |-------+-------+-------+-------+-------+-------|
  * |  LShif  |   Z   |   X   |   C   |   V   |   B   |--------|    |--------|   N   |   M   |   ,   |   .   |   /   | RShif |
  * `-------------------------------------------------/        /    \        \-----------------------------------------------'
  *            |  LGUI  |  LAlt  |  LCTR  |  LOWER  |/  Enter /      \  Space \|  RAISE  |  RCTR  |  RAlt  |  RGUI  |
  *            |        |        |        |         /        /        \        \         |        |        |        |
  *            `-----------------------------------''-------'          '-------''-----------------------------------'
